Vidyasiri

The Government of Karnataka has introduced the Vidyasiri Scholarship Scheme 2022 for SC/ST/OBC/PWD students. Through this ePass Vidyasiri scholarship, post-matric students who are unable to pay for their higher education are encouraged to enrol in their courses of choice. The government has also made available the ePass (electronic payment and scholarship application system) service to make it simple for students to apply for and receive Vidyasiri scholarships

Vidhyadhan

Vidyadhan Scholarship Program from Sarojini Damodaran Foundation supports the college education of meritorious students from economically challenged families. The students are selected after completion of 10th grade /SSLC through a rigorous selection process including test and interview. Currently Vidyadhan program has around 5000 students across the following states: Kerala, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Pondicherry, Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Telangana, Goa, Odisha, New Delhi and Ladhak. Those selected will be eligible for two year scholarship from the Foundation. If they continue to do well, they will be given scholarship for pursuing any degree course of their interest; these scholarships are directly through the foundation or external sponsors who have registered with the Foundation. The scholarship amount for graduation courses varies from Rs 10,000 to Rs 60,000 per year depending on the state, course, duration etc. The selected students will be required to attend the mentoring programs from the Foundation. Students can apply free of cost on the website direclty. No other person or institutions have been authorized to select students on our behalf.

SSP

SSP scholarship is an umbrella term for the scholarships offered by the Government of Karnataka for its domicile students on its State Scholarship Portal (SSP). It is a dedicated online platform run by the Government of Karnataka to ensure effective implementation of a number of pre-matric and post-matric scholarships offered by different government departments. The key objective of this scholarship is to financially support those meritorious students who face financial difficulties in pursuing their education irrespective of their caste, category or gender.
